## Findings
-  partylist-25 and senate-25 have a wide dataset type and they contain more information regarding votes (including valid ballots, over votes, under votes, and valid votes).
as compraed to philippines 2025_elections which has it confined to one row.
- Keep in mind that partylist-25 and senate-25 have less records than philippines_2025_election_results. This should be treated more as a general summary
than an accurate measure of total election voting.
